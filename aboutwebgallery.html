WEB GALLERY is an online art display for a photographic alternation service that provides image adjustments in order to gain feedback. The pictures taken are captured on foot and adjusted in order to create an alternative look. The program used to apply adjustments to the pictures creates several alternatives, allowing for many selections to be chosen. Any picture can be alternated, so existing pictures can be altered as well as a picture requested. To have pictures taken or alternated, please refer to CONTACT in the navigation bar and provide your information. The web gallery is open for viewing and downloads will be provided in the future.
