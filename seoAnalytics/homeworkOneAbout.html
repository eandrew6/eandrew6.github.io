This will be my first web page created for a service and the service I would like to provide is photography. I have taken several pictures and I like to apply image adjustments to the pictures taken for artistic purposes. I would like to create a photo gallery, gather feedback of the pictures I have taken so far and provide a contact in order to provide photo alternations and gather additional feedback. I plan to continue taking more pictures to add to the gallery and create a service plan.

Erica Andrews is a photography service that provides image adjustment in order to gain feedback. The pictures taken are captured on foot and adjusted in order to create an alternative look. The program used to apply adjustments to the pictures creates several alternatives, allowing for many selections to be chosen. Any picture can be alternated, so existing pictures can be altered as well as a picture requested. To have pictures taken or alternated, please refer to the contact page and provide your contact information. The photo gallery is open for viewing and downloads will be provided in the future.
