'Erica Andrews' is a photography service that provides image adjustment in order to gain feedback. The pictures taken are captured on foot and adjusted in order to create an alternative look. The program used to apply adjustments to the pictures creates several alternatives, allowing for many selections to be chosen. Any picture can be alternated, so existing pictures can be altered as well as a pictures requested. To have pictures taken or alternated, please refer to the contact link above and provide your contact information. The photo gallery is open for viewing and downloads will be provided in the future.
