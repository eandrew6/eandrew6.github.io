THE PINK PANTHER

Wikipedia: The Pink Panther

According to Wikipedia, the Pink Panther is a character featured on a series of comedy films. Beginning in 1963, the Pink Panther provided inspiration for other media such as comic books and animated series. The Pink Panther is described to be a very valuable, large "pink diamond" known as the "Pink Panther". The name of the diamond comes from the diamonds appearance when looked at closely, resembling a "leaping pink panther".
