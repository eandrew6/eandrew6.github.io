MARIO

Wikipedia: Mario

According to Wikipedia, Mario is a video game, fictional character that was created by a Shigeru Miyamoto of the company known as Nintendo. This popular, video game character is known world wide and has appeared in over two hundred video games thus far. The Mario character description is said to be a "pudgy and short Italian plumber", living in the Mushroom Kingdom where he ventures toward rescue missions involving a Princess Peach from a villian named "the Koopa Bowser". This character also has a sidekick who happens to be his younger brother known as "Luigi".
