Bugs Bunny

Wikipedia: Bugs Bunny

According to Wikipedia, the animated character known as Bugs Bunny was created by a Leon Schlesinger Productions in 1940 and later created by Warner Bros. Cartoons, starring in animated short films such as the "Looney Tunes" and the "Merrie Melodies" produced by Warner Bros. The character is described as a gray hare famous for having a "worry-free" personality. With a New York accent, this "trickster" typically is shown chewing a carrot and speaking his popular catch phrase, "Eh... What's up, doc?"
